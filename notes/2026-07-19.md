### 📊 My own analysis: Nvidia (NVDA)

Nvidia makes most of its money from producing chips. Its biggest advantage is the dominance it has over other chip producers, which gives Nvidia pricing power. This is because Nvidia is so big and has technology that other companies don't have. The main risk is that once other companies can produce similar chips, Nvidia will lose its dominance in the market.
