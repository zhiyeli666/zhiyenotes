### ✍️ Reflection

The stock still fell even though the deliveries were impressive. People don't only want to see cars; with the big spending and nearly negative $10 billion cash flow, they need to see proof, not just promises. Another possible explanation is "buy the rumor, sell the news": the stock had already risen while people were predicting strong deliveries. The stock rose later when a safety investigation ended, because removing a "risk" reduces the chance of a big fall, which makes investors more comfortable buying the stock.
