### ✍️ Reflection

I think semiconductors are hard to make, and the technology is concentrated in a few companies like Nvidia, which allows those firms to earn huge profits. However, software technology is more common, so the profits that software companies should be making end up flowing to semiconductor companies. This profit gap will eventually narrow when more companies successfully manufacture semiconductors. The delayed IPO of a huge AI company shows the instability of AI-related stocks, which is why investors are worried about the whole AI industry.
