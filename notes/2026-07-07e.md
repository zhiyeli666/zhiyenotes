### ✍️ Reflection

LETFs are more suitable for the short term; they eat into your money when volatility happens. For example, if a normal stock rises 10% then falls 10%, it stays almost flat, but the 2x ETF ends up losing money. In conclusion, it is designed for short periods, not long ones.
