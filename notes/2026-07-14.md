### ✍️ Reflection

Oil prices rise on the fear of disruption because nobody wants their factories to stop for lack of fuel, so to avoid this problem, people buy oil in advance, which causes the price to rise as more people buy. Higher oil prices are bad for the overall stock market because the costs for most companies go up, which could lead to worse cash flow.
