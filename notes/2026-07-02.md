---

### ✍️ Reflection

Most people believed that computing power and chips were very rare and in huge demand. But Meta's plan to sell AI computing power shows that it has more computing power than it needs for its own AI models. As a result, people are panicking and selling their chip stocks. Chip makers are producing more chips than the big AI companies actually need, which could lead to a sharp drop in chip makers' stock prices. A drop in chip prices might even make AI companies' stocks rise, since they would have lower costs.
