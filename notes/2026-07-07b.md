### ✍️ Reflection

Buying a big company like NBCUniversal takes a lot of money. Rather than buying it, growing on your own might be better. Netflix is already making its own shows, which requires less money than buying a huge company and greatly reduces the risk. So this explains why Netflix's stock went up when it announced it would not buy NBCUniversal.
