### ✍️ Reflection

Samsung's stock fell after a record profit because the profit was already priced into the stock. After the real news came, the stock fell instead — this is known as "buy the rumor, sell the news." Another reason might be that Samsung is planning to invest 400 trillion won in a new semiconductor manufacturing hub. This is similar to what happened to Tesla's stock after its record deliveries.
